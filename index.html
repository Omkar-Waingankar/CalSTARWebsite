WE ARE: CALSTAR

UC BERKELEY'S SPACE TECHNOLOGY AND ROCKETRY TEAM

WELCOME

CalSTAR is UC Berkeley’s sole premier competitive rocketry team. Founded in Fall 2015 in a university without an aerospace engineering program, CalSTAR is comprised of a medley of audacious and wildly curious students with a burning passion for space.

WHAT WE DO

REDEFINING COLLEGIATE SPACE EXPLORATION

COMPETE

This coming year, CalSTAR aspires to compete in NASA's Student Launch Competition and achieve a Space Shot, a feat that no other college has accomplished, in the upcoming years.

MEMBERS

As a member, one will obtain industry-level hands-on experience with the entire design process -- from inception and design, through manufacturing and integration, and all the way to testing and launching.

OUTREACH

In addition to pursuing competitions, CalSTAR aims to educate local Bay Area students and residents about rocketry to foster a growing aerospace community.

CalSTAR dares to reach where no other college team has been before.
