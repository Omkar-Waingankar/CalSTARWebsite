WE ARE: CALSTAR

UC BERKELEY'S SPACE TECHNOLOGY AND ROCKETRY TEAM

WELCOME

CalSTAR is UC Berkeley’s premier competitive rocketry team. Founded in Fall 2015 in a university without an aerospace engineering program, CalSTAR is comprised of a medley of audacious and wildly curious students with a burning passion for space.

WHAT WE DO

Redefining collegiate space exploration.

COMPETE

Reach new heights in NASA's Student Launch Competition.

MEMBERS

Gain industry experience and make lifelong friendships.

OUTREACH

Educate local Bay Area students and residents about aerospace.
